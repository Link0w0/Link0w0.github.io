Description

Project "Protect Penguin" was a task I did as a part of Google's UX Designer program. I created UX for a new app and responsive website to help people support the protection of endangered penguin species. The target users would be people that are interested in wildlife and penguins. The app and website will include news and facts how to help protect penguins and options to adopt a penguin or donate money.
